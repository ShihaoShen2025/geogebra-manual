OdstranitelnaNespojitost( <Funkce> )::

Computes the removable discontinuity at a point for broken rational functions (also for previews).

[EXAMPLE]
====

`++RemovableDiscontinuity((3-x)/(2 x^(2)-6 x))++` yields _(3,-0.17)_.

====
